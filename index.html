Battleships

1. Place all five battleships on the grid by selecting each of the boats below and a position on the grid
2. Ships can be rotated by clicking on the direction button or using the spacebar
3. Click the play button to go into battle with the computer
4. Find the ships on the computer's board by clicking on the squares in the grid
5. Click on all parts of the boat to successfully sink it
6. Find all the ships before the computer to win and beat the computer!
